# Code of Conduct

We want IssueLab to be a welcoming, respectful place for collaboration.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Disagree on ideas, not on people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Disruptive behavior that blocks collaboration.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the
maintainers via GitHub and mark the report as conduct-related.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to keep the
community safe and productive.
